About Us

Derek & Elizabeth McGee founded SunChase Construction in Pensacola, FL in 1991. Derek was raised by a construction business owner and was taught an extremely strong work ethic at a very early age. Derek spent 20+ years running a successful construction business, specializing in masonry sub-contracting work for large commercial projects before he stepped away from the construction industry as a whole and opened up ‘Oak Ridge Gun Range Gulf Breeze’ in 2013.

Four years after stepping away from the construction business, Derek’s youngest son, Kyle, moved back to Gulf Breeze after graduating Florida State University with degrees in Finance & Management & began a career in Real Estate. One year after starting his career in Real Estate, Kyle began to develop a passion for Real Estate Development. Alongside his family’s extensive construction background, Kyle pursued & obtained his General Contractor’s license and found some property to build his first spec home with his father, Derek. Unfortunately, just some 8 months after the two began working together, Derek was diagnosed with cancer & 5 months after he was diagnosed he lost his battle with cancer.

Kyle is a Gulf Breeze native and along with his success as a property developer, he has also built up a successful Real Estate business as a realtor with Levin Rinke Realty. He is extremely motivated to build & continue his father’s legacy through SunChase Construction & he is currently focused on building appealing, high quality residential homes in the greater Pensacola area.
