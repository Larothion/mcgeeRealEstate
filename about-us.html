About Us

Samuel Stevens & Associates construction was founded by two brothers, Steve and Sam Dougherty. Later another relative (by marriage) joined the clan. Bill Heslin is a retired Air Force Lt. Col., and has been in construction since 1986. Steve is a Real Estate Broker/Associate and contractor. Sam is a former advertising executive and residential contractor. Since our company opened its doors in 2009, we’ve treated every customer like they were a part of our family. Other companies may offer similar services, but our services come with a guarantee and a personal touch!
